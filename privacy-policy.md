# Privacy Policy for Crateline

**Last Updated: June 13, 2026**

## Overview

Crateline ("the App") is a personal vinyl record collection management application. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or have access to any personal information about you.** Crateline does not require account creation, login, or any personal identifiers.

### Camera Access

The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes on vinyl record packaging. The camera feed is processed locally on your device to detect barcode data. **No images, video, or camera data are stored, saved, or transmitted anywhere.**

### Collection Data

Information about records you add to your collection (such as album title, artist, year, label, format, genre, and cover art image links) is stored **locally on your device only**, using your device's local storage. This data:

- Is never transmitted to us
- Is never transmitted to any server we control
- Can be deleted by you at any time by removing items from your collection or uninstalling the App
- Is not backed up by us (you are responsible for any backups via your device's own backup systems, such as iCloud)

## Third-Party Services

When you scan a barcode, the App sends the scanned barcode number to the **Discogs API** (operated by Zink Media, Inc.) to retrieve publicly available release information (album title, artist, year, label, genre, cover art, etc.).

- Only the barcode number is transmitted — no information about you or your device is sent beyond what is technically necessary for the request (such as standard network information).
- We do not control how Discogs handles this data. Please review the Discogs Privacy Policy at: https://www.discogs.com/privacy

## Data Security

Because all collection data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data depends on your device's own security features (passcode, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date and using a passcode or biometric lock.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no personal information is collected at all, the App does not pose the data-collection risks that privacy laws like COPPA are designed to address.

## Your Choices

- You can deny camera permission, though this will prevent barcode scanning (a core feature of the App).
- You can delete any or all items from your collection at any time within the App.
- You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App from your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this same URL with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
